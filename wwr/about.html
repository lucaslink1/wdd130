At Dry Oar Rafting Co. we believe that the best memories are made on the river. Our purpose is to create safe, thrilling, and unforgettable white water experiences for families, friends, and adventurers of all levels.

History

Dry Oar Rafting Co. started as a small weekend hobby and slowly grew into a full-time passion project. Over the years, we have guided countless guests down winding rivers, roaring rapids, and peaceful canyons. Our story is one of adventure, community, and a shared love for wild water and wild places.
